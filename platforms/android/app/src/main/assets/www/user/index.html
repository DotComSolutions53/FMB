Faiz ul Mawaid il Burhaniyah

Burhani Masjid - Kolkata

Every mumin would gladly give up a fortune for a morsel from his Moula’s thaal. He holds in his heart the utmost conviction that this morsel, be it a grain of rice or a crumb of bread, is a cause of immense barakat..
What if this honor was bestowed upon him every day? Not only obtain a tiny morsel, but an entire meal? What if he spent his days and nights replete with the food from the thaal of his Moula TUS? What could then be said of the boundless barakaat encompassing his life, his home, his family? He could never dream of it let only imagine.
Only from a phenomenal being can come such a phenomenon. A wish that was beyond dreams, past a mumin’s farthest wishes today is a reality- not only a reality, but a way of life.
The name of this phenomenon?
Faiz ul Mawaid il Burhaniyah
